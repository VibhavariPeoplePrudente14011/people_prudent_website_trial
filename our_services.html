Our Services

People Prudent Consulting excels in helping clients establish effective People Policies, Systems, and Processes for greater business success.
Keeping clients' tangible returns on investment as one of the critical yardsticks for measuring its success, People Prudent offers the following services:

Total Rewards Solutions

At People Prudent, we take pride in our expertise in crafting and implementing comprehensive Total Rewards solutions for our clients. From designing salary scales to devising short and long-term incentive programs, we deliver customized schemes tailored to meet the specific reward needs of each company. Our approach goes beyond one-size-fits-all solutions, focusing instead on a holistic Total Rewards strategy grounded in deep technical knowledge and well-researched tools.

Business HR Diagnostics

In today's fast-paced business environment, it's easy for organizations to lose sight of the value of their human capital. At People Prudent, we help companies refocus on their most valuable asset by conducting thorough diagnostics of their People Processes. By identifying key areas for improvement, we guide organizations in reinventing and revitalizing themselves to unlock their full potential.

HR Strategy Development

Our team at People Prudent is dedicated to assisting clients in developing robust HR strategies tailored to their unique business needs. Through this high-impact, value-added service, we align all human capital processes, activities, and initiatives with the organization's overarching business strategy.

Organization Design

At People Prudent, we specialize in crafting organizational structures that seamlessly align with your business strategy. Using proven methodologies and engaging stakeholders at every step, we create agile frameworks tailored to your company's unique characteristics, industry dynamics, and long-term objectives. The result is an organization design that is geared to perform optimally today, is agile, and is ready for the challenges of the future.

Performance Enhancement

As one of the core areas of expertise, People Prudent helps organizations set-up effective Performance management systems. We link it from Strategy Deployment, Goal Cascading, Performance Tracking, Performance coaching, to Reward mechanisms. Our bespoke, designed with, and for the clients, Performance Management Processes enables superior business performance for our clients.

Job Design, Descriptions, Sizing

We provide support in defining new jobs, redesigning existing ones, creating job descriptions and internal job hierarchies. Our expertise ensures that job roles are clearly defined, sized appropriately, and aligned with organizational objectives.

Start-up HR Support

Start-ups face unique HR challenges, and our team is here to provide comprehensive support during this crucial early stage of operation. From setting up organizational structures to designing job roles and implementing HR policies and systems, we ensure a smooth and effective transition into business growth.

People Matters Insights

People Prudent carries out regular surveys on relevant HR/People matters in the region so as to create meaningful insights for the HR fraternity and for business leaders. In addition, we help clients carry out bespoke surveys around people matters that are of significance to them. The client commissioned surveys are fully tailormade to address client-specific requirements and hence, if needed, they are scoped around select industry, talent pool, or other parameters of stratification as required by the client.

Retainer Services - Recruitment, Executive Search, and more...

In addition to our core services, we offer HR retainer services across various categories, including recruitment, executive search, advisory services, organization development, and active engagement on client HR committees. We remain committed to guiding and supporting our clients every step of the way, ensuring sustained success and growth.
